Namaste, I'm Vinita!

I'm a designer and I aim to provide good user experience while keeping in mind accessibility, usability and sustainability of the design. I spend most of my time subconsciously finding examples of good and bad designs around me. Whenever I'm using any new application/product I just want to know all the functionalities and interactions. I want to know what they had in mind while designing that particular product.

I got my bachelors degree in Computer Science and now I'm pursuing Masters in Human-Computer Interaction at RIT. I like to visit new places and to meet new people. I'm an active member of RIT Storytellers club and Rochester UpState UX Group. Apart from this, I play basketball, paint and read books! When I'm not doing any of these things, I'm managing my time to fit everything in my schedule!
